Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my financial data?

We use bank-grade 256-bit AES encryption for all data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Your security is our highest priority, utilizing multi-factor authentication and regular third-party audits.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your dashboard. If you upgrade, the new features are available immediately, and pricing will be prorated.

What happens after my Professional trial?

You'll have 14 days to explore all Professional features. If you choose not to subscribe, your account will automatically transition to the Basic plan without losing any historical data.
